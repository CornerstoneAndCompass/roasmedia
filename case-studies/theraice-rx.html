About TheraICE Rx

TheraICE Rx manufactures cold compression therapy products including headache relief caps, hot and cold compression sleeves, and wrist ice packs. The company ranks as the #1 seller on Amazon in their category and historically focused primarily on that platform, which provided a consistent revenue stream but limited growth potential.

When TheraICE Rx partnered with ROAS Media in May 2022, they had attempted Meta and Google advertising with minimal budgets in the past but struggled, discontinuing Meta for several years. The brand needed a fresh approach to diversify beyond Amazon and unlock new growth channels on Shopify and other platforms.

The Challenge

Rebuilding from Zero Ad Account Data

The Meta ad account had minimal historical data since the business was primarily built on Amazon. Two months before engaging ROAS Media, TheraICE Rx ran small Meta tests with nearly zero return, creating skepticism about the channel's potential. The challenge was to completely rebuild the entire funnel structure, overhaul strategies and creative assets, while simultaneously monitoring both Shopify and Amazon impacts to ensure the growth didn't cannibalize existing revenue.

Starting from a position of near-zero profitability on paid channels required a methodical, test-driven approach with careful budget allocation and constant performance monitoring.

Our Strategy

A Four-Month Scaling Playbook

We implemented a phased approach over four months, gradually scaling proven tactics while continuously testing new opportunities:

  • Month 1 - Foundation & Creative Testing: Built a full-funnel campaign structure from scratch, tested 30+ ad creatives across Meta, discovered positive halo effect on Amazon sales, and moved the account from near-zero ROAS to breakeven
  • Month 2 - Scaling & Conversion Optimization: Doubled ad spend, ran landing page split testing, tested product bundles for higher average order value, and partnered with Chronos Agency on CRM optimization
  • Months 3-4 - Refinement & Multi-Channel Expansion: Allocated 70-80% of budget to proven performers and 20-30% to testing, achieved profitable ROAS across all funnel layers, and expanded to additional paid channels
The Results

Multi-Six-Figure Monthly Revenue

By months 3-4, we achieved profitable ROAS at every funnel level with consistent success across all paid channels. The 518% increase from baseline monthly Shopify sales demonstrated the power of a systematic approach to scaling. Revenue doubled in month 1 and nearly doubled again in month 2, proving the potential of paid advertising for the brand.

The partnership continued to scale beyond initial projections, expanding to multi-six-figure monthly revenue figures and growing internationally beyond the US market, demonstrating the repeatability and scalability of the model across geographies.
