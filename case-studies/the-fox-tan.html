Brand Story

About The Fox Tan

The Fox Tan is an Australian-based, female-owned tanning accelerator brand founded by Olivia Zorzut and Kelly Euripidou. Their innovative products contain active amino acids designed to help customers achieve their tanning goals with less sun exposure. Successfully scaling in Australia since 2017, the brand had built a loyal customer base and trusted reputation. Ready for the next chapter, The Fox Tan aimed to expand internationally into the northern hemisphere markets where brand awareness and promotional infrastructure didn't yet exist.

The Challenge

Scaling Without Paid Influencers

The Fox Tan had built its success on authentic customer testimonials and grassroots word-of-mouth marketing. They had never relied on paid influencers or traditional advertising approaches. Now, expanding into unfamiliar international markets without existing brand awareness or promotional infrastructure presented a new challenge: how to adapt their authentic, community-driven approach for audiences that had never heard of the brand.

The team needed a strategy that preserved their brand authenticity while achieving rapid scale in new territories where they had no footprint.

Our Strategy

Community-First Expansion Playbook

By analyzing customer engagement patterns, we discovered a powerful insight: customers were frequently asking about salon usage in ad comments, revealing an entirely untapped market segment. We built our strategy around this discovery and three key pillars:

  • Discovered customers frequently asked about salon usage in ad comments, revealing untapped market segment
  • Emphasized "Trusted by over 100,000+ babes worldwide" messaging to establish credibility in new markets
  • Addressed bounce rates by adding specific shipping timeframes per market, reducing purchase friction
  • Employed manual budget scaling every 30-60 minutes using global day and night teams to capitalize on daily algorithm momentum
  • Created salon-focused creative that resonated with professional beauty professionals
  • Maintained authentic customer testimonial approach while expanding to paid channels
The Results

$1M in Monthly Revenue from One Market

Through our salon-focused creative and rapid optimization approach, The Fox Tan delivered over AUD $1 million in trackable revenue in a single month. The strategy unlocked an entirely new customer segment while maintaining the brand's authentic voice.

Cross-border expansion drove exceptional growth: +405% YoY revenue increase and +213% growth in new customer acquisition from international markets. What started as a local Australian brand success story became a global phenomenon, proving that authenticity combined with strategic paid media can achieve unprecedented scale.
