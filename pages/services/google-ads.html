Google Advertising

Google Ads for the High-Intent Customer

When someone searches for what you sell, they're ready to buy. Google captures those exact moments at scale. We manage the full Google ecosystem -Search, Shopping, Display, and YouTube -to turn browsers into buyers and maximize every dollar invested.

The Opportunity

Capture Customers When They're Ready to Buy

Google isn't like other channels. Your audience is actively searching for solutions. That means higher intent, better quality conversions, and the chance to capture customers you'd never reach on brand awareness channels. But only if you're set up correctly.

Are you struggling to maintain your return on ad spend while increasing spend?

Most brands hit a ceiling with Google Ads around $20k-$40k per month in ad spend. The problem isn't Google—it's that when you try to scale, your ROAS drops because you're bidding inefficiently, your targeting widens too much, and your messaging doesn't adapt to new audiences.

That's where ROAS Media comes in. We've helped brands scale past that ceiling by fixing bid strategies, tightening audience targeting, and optimizing every layer of your Google Ads system. Growth doesn't have to come with a drop in profitability.

Data Driven Decision Making

Every brand is unique. What works for a DTC fashion brand won't work for a SaaS company. That's why we don't use templates. We audit your data, understand your unit economics, and build a strategy that's specific to your business. Every keyword bid, every ad copy test, every feed adjustment is rooted in data about what actually drives profitable customers for you.

You're Bidding Wrong

Keyword bidding on Google isn't intuitive. Most businesses either overbid (burning cash on expensive keywords) or underbid (missing customers). Manual bidding doesn't scale. And Google's automated bidding works great for Google's revenue, not necessarily for your profitability.

Your Product Feed is Costing You Sales

Shopping ads depend entirely on feed quality. Poor product titles, missing descriptions, bad images -and your products never appear. Or they appear but the feed is so bloated you're paying for low-intent searches that convert poorly.

You're Not Using All the Channels

Most brands run Search or Shopping but ignore YouTube and Display. You're leaving money on the table. YouTube reaches people considering solutions. Display reaches people actively researching. Together with Search, they build a full-funnel machine.

You Can't See Real Performance

Google's attribution is opaque. Without proper conversion tracking and server-side implementation, you're making decisions on incomplete data. Clicks don't equal revenue. You need to see which keywords and products actually drive profitable customers.
