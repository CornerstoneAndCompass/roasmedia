Brand Story

About Pipsticks

Pipsticks launched in 2014 by Mo Vazquez as a sticker club side project that evolved into a thriving online and offline business. The brand focuses on creating delightful, aesthetically pleasing products designed to spark joy and wonder in customers.

With a unique blend of subscription and e-commerce revenue models, Pipsticks had grown into a beloved lifestyle brand serving thousands of customers globally. The challenge was optimizing ad spend across both business models while maintaining healthy profitability.

The Challenge

Complex Data, Strategic Complexity

Pipsticks operated a dual revenue model combining subscription services through Recharge with transactional e-commerce on Shopify. This created three interconnected problems:

  • Lack of reporting tools to handle complex subscription-plus-transactional data
  • Difficulty managing inconsistencies across Shopify, Recharge, and operations data sources
  • Strategic complexity of balancing ad spend between subscription and one-off purchase campaigns without clear visibility into true customer acquisition costs

Without unified analytics, it was impossible to optimize campaigns effectively or understand which customer cohorts were truly profitable.

Our Strategy

Building a Custom Analytics Foundation

We designed a comprehensive analytics and optimization system specifically for Pipsticks' dual revenue model:

  • Built custom analytics dashboard integrating Recharge and Shopify data
  • Tracked active subscriptions, new subscriber numbers, one-off purchase revenue, and categorized ad spend
  • Established clear KPIs aligned with business profitability
  • Implemented custom Meta conversions using fixed CLTV values for daily optimization
  • Tracked Overall ROAS, New Subscriber CAC, and Expected ROAS projections
  • Optimized campaigns around subscriber acquisition value while maintaining one-off purchase profitability
The Results

Data-Driven Growth Across All Channels

With the new analytics framework in place, we achieved significant improvements across both business models:

  • 26% reduction in customer acquisition costs through precision targeting and optimization
  • 106% monthly increase in one-off product sales through optimized product campaigns
  • 35% monthly growth in new subscriptions through refined subscriber acquisition strategy

The analytical rigor of the approach resonated deeply with Pipsticks' leadership, particularly Nathan (partner), whose Wall Street trading background gave him deep appreciation for the data-driven methodology and clear KPI tracking.
