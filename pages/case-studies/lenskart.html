About Lenskart

Founded in 2010, Lenskart is a multi-national eyewear retail chain valued at over $4.5 billion with heavyweight investors such as Softbank and TPG Capital. In 2022, Lenskart acquired a majority stake in Japanese direct-to-consumer eyewear brand Owndays, helping to create one of Asia's largest omni-channel eyewear retailers.

Lenskart joined the ROAS Media portfolio in September 2020 and set aggressive goals to reach most of the Singapore population within Q4 of 2020, while driving improvement on overall ROI and scaling ad spend by 50% month-on-month.

The Challenge

Disrupting Singapore's Offline Shopping Culture

Most consumers in Singapore are used to shopping for optical wear offline. Shopping at the various mega malls within Singapore is almost a national sport. With an eCommerce website and a mobile application, Lenskart needed to disrupt the shopping norm and identify the right advertising strategy to drive online purchases while at the same time, encouraging retail sales and supporting new retail store launches.

Website and app conversion rates in Singapore were initially too low to hit sales targets, requiring collaboration on localization strategies and conversion rate optimization.

Our Strategy

A Multi-Channel Scaling Playbook

We segmented the audience into three distinct groups - retail, online, and app - to maximize success and prevent channel cannibalization across each.

  • Deployed hyper-targeted ads near retail locations during store opening hours to support new store launches
  • Launched fresh creatives every few days to maintain ad performance during aggressive scaling
  • Implemented continuous daily and hourly campaign optimization, particularly on weekends
  • Operated our media buying team 7 days a week to capitalize on peak purchase periods
  • Ran different promotional deals across retail, online, and app channels to prevent revenue cannibalization
  • Collaborated on localization strategies and CRO to improve conversion rates in the Singapore market
The Results

498% Revenue Increase in Year One

As a result of our multi-pronged approach and rapid scaling across all 3 channels, within the first year of partnering together, we helped Lenskart to achieve a 498% increase in revenue.

Through our split-testing of online and offline approaches, we also managed to fine-tune the strategies and promotional planning to streamline new store openings and provide key marketing support to enable successful new store launches.
