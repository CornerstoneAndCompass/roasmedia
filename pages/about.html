Our Story

From Facebook HQ to Your Growth Partner

After scaling some of the biggest eCommerce brands in the APAC region while at Facebook, Rich saw a gap in the market. Brands needed an agency that operated like an internal team - deeply embedded, fully transparent, and obsessively focused on return on ad spend.

That insight became ROAS Media. Since then, we've grown into one of the largest independent performance agencies in APAC, with a global team operating across the USA, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, and Malaysia.

We've helped our clients generate over $250 million in trackable advertising revenue. But the number we're most proud of? The brands who started with us as scrappy startups and are now category leaders.

Our reputation and results drive our business. We don't rely on slick sales pitches - we let the numbers speak for themselves. If your brand is ready to scale, we'll show you exactly how we'll get you there. If it's not the right time, we'll tell you that too.
