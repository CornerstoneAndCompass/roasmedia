Our Approach

Building Your TikTok Ads System

We've invested time understanding TikTok's algorithm, platform culture, and what actually drives conversions. Here's how we build profitable TikTok campaigns:

🎬 Native Creative Design

TikTok's algorithm rewards content that feels native to the platform. Polished brand videos fail. We create UGC-style ads that feel authentic and stop the scroll:

  • ✓ Hook-driven creative (first 3 seconds are critical)
  • ✓ UGC-style (user-generated content) aesthetics
  • ✓ Platform-native vertical video format
  • ✓ Trending sounds and templates (strategically used)
  • ✓ Quick cuts and fast-paced editing that matches platform norms

🎯 Audience Targeting Strategy

TikTok's audience targeting is sophisticated. We build a layered targeting approach:

  • ✓ Interest-based targeting (specific to your niche)
  • ✓ Behavioral targeting (user actions on platform)
  • ✓ Lookalike audiences (from your converters)
  • ✓ Retargeting (website visitors, catalog viewers)
  • ✓ Broad campaigns with good creative (let algorithm find audiences)

🛍️ TikTok Shop Integration

TikTok Shop lets you sell directly on the platform. We set it up and optimize it:

  • ✓ Product catalog setup and optimization
  • ✓ Shoppable video creation
  • ✓ Pricing and inventory management
  • ✓ Spark Ads that amplify organic content
  • ✓ End-to-end TikTok Shop campaigns

📊 Conversion Tracking & Optimization

You need to know what actually works. We implement proper tracking:

  • ✓ TikTok pixel implementation (on-site conversions)
  • ✓ Server-side conversion tracking (more accurate)
  • ✓ Real revenue attribution (not just clicks)
  • ✓ Continuous A/B testing (creative, targeting, landing pages)
  • ✓ CPA and ROAS optimization
